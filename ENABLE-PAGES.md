# ⚠️ Enable GitHub Pages (do this once)

Your site will show **404** until you turn on Pages in GitHub. Use **only** "Deploy from a branch" (not "GitHub Actions") to avoid duplicate builds and cancelation errors.

---

## 3 steps

1. **Open:** https://github.com/biggleem/nc-clicker-game-v1 **→ click "Settings"** (top tab).

2. **Left sidebar → "Pages"** (under Code and automation).

3. **Under "Build and deployment":**
   - **Source:** choose **"Deploy from a branch"** (do not choose "GitHub Actions").
   - **Branch:** choose **"main"**.
   - **Folder:** choose **"/ (root)"**.
   - Click **"Save"**.

---

## Then

- Wait **2–5 minutes**.
- Open: **https://biggleem.github.io/nc-clicker-game-v1/**
- If the repo is **Private**, make it **Public** first (Settings → General → Danger zone), or Pages will not work on a free account.
